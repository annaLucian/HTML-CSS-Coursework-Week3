At angel cupcake we love cupcakes above all things. Well, the pastries, the sweets, the coffees, those that are made with our love and dedication. When you discover our cupcake menu, the same thing will happen to you: ANGEL CAKE'S DELIGHTS WILL BE YOUR LIFE.

Come to worship your favorite delicacies at any time of the day. If your body asks you for a cupcake when you wake up, open our breakfast menu and satisfy your fantasies. What are you more than breakfast brunch? In that case, you might be more in the mood to try our focaccia or our savory savory flavors. And if what you want is to spend the afternoon in one of our places, why not ask for a coffee with a new cupcake flavor?

We have proposals to surprise you. Choose between sweet or salty. Savor our chocolate, cheese or carrot cakes or our apple crisps. Enjoy a Belgian chocolate muffin, an ensaimada or a fruit cupcake. If you prefer salt, our Galician tortilla, tuna or Iberian ham are waiting for you. And don't skip the quiches or say no to the crystal salmon and avocado burger!

Top it off with a hot or cold drink. An horchata or an American coffee? A detox smoothie or a coconut frappe? Natural lemonade or a red tea? The choice is yours! Come to see us.
